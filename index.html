Juniorpreneur is the world's first modular storefront - an evolution of the classic lemonade stand - that makes it fun and engaging for your child to develop life-long entrepreneurial skills.

The kit comes with our six-module learning program to guide your junior entrepreneur through the basics of building a business plan, setting goals, creating a brand, experimenting with marketing and tracking their progress.

Juniorpreneur lets your child harness their natural entrepreneurial spirit, while gaining invaluable experience through the Juniorpreneur learning program.
