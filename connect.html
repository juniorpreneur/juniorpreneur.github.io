Meet The
Dads Behind
Juniorpreneur

A former national champion in bobsled, author and serial entrepreneur, Jayson has a knack for spotting opportunities and bringing people together to make things real.

Today, Jayson is a father of two developing entrepreneurs and runs a boutique consulting firm that provides innovative solutions to accelerate the development of leaders inside organizations.

Entrepreneurship runs deep in Gavin's DNA, having grown up in a household where both parents were self-employed and entrepreneurship was a way of life. Gavin opened his first brick-and-mortar business while still in high school. Later selling his business to start a new company in the graphics industry, which grew to have eight offices nationally and a staff of over seventy employees. In 2010, he was named a 'Leader of Tomorrow' by Business Calgary Magazine.

Today, Gavin is the father of two remarkable girls and is an active partner in a number of business ventures. He works with entrepreneurs in all stages of their business, helping to build their strategic plan using his exclusive ATLAS program to facilitate the process.

Creating, designing, and building is Daniel's bread and butter, seeing as he grew up in a house that his father is still trying to complete to this day! Daniel started swinging a hammer before he could walk and spent his entire childhood helping his father build the family dream home. After college, Daniel spent 12 years in the church as a youth director teaching and leading children of all ages. During his tenure at the church he had to make quite a few lemonade stands for the vacation bible school programs in the summer and always thought there had to be a better way.

Today, Daniel is a first time father with a healthy newborn baby boy in Calgary. He works with a local non-profit in a town that helps professional athlentes and para athletes mentor classroom children all over North America
